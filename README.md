## 📖 About

This portfolio serves as my digital resume and creative showcase. It highlights:

- **Who I am** – a brief bio and professional background.
- **My work** – featured projects with descriptions, tech stacks, and links.
- **My skills** – technologies, tools, and competencies.
- **Contact** – ways to get in touch (email, social links, etc.).

The design focuses on simplicity, readability, and a cohesive personal brand identity.
